The Early Life of Thunder 

After actress, Thunder was born on May 19, 1994, in Spokane, Washington, USA. She belongs to Native American, Black, and Spanish descent. 

She grew up in the street of her hometown under the guidance of her single mother. Thunder's mother raised her and her sister with a lot of love and care. 

Interestingly, Thunder started caring for her hair when she was five. 

Thunder Didn't Always Have Curly Hair

You may have always seen the attractive and hot model Thunder with curly hair. However, that was not always the case for her. In an interview with BIRDIE, she stated that her hair was straight and all she wanted was curly hair. 

She cut her hair and made it shorter when she turned thirteen, and since then, her hair has started going super curly. During that interview, Thunder also gave some tips on hair and how to properly take care of your hair.

Career Overview

As mentioned earlier, Thunder was attracted to the glamour industry from an early age and wanted to be a model. After finishing her high school education, she started clicking photos and giving auditions in order to get a contract.

Through her talent in modelling and her unmatched beauty, she became a well-known face in the modelling industry in a concise period of time. She started working as a model for various weekly and monthly magazines such as Glamour, Allure, Teen Vogue, and WSJ.
