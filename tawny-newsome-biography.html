Who is Tawny Newsome?

Tawny Newsome is an American actress and writer who has acted in many screen projects. Tawny Newsome is popular for featuring in Spies in Disguise, Space Force, Vacation Friends, Brockmire, etc.

In 2023, she was seen in the role of Mercedes in the comedy movie Quiz Lady along with other stars named Awkwafina, Sandra Oh, Jason Schwartzman, and Holland Taylor.

This film has got a fair response from the audience as it has ratings of 6.8 on IMDb and only 79 percent on Rotten Tomatoes.

Her upcoming screen projects My Neighbors Are Dead as Peggy Pathmark, Star Trek: Lower Decks as Beckett Mariner (voice), and Nuked Pos are in the post-production stage and will be released soon.
