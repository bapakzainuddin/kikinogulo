Who is Ami Mcclure?

Nigerian Ami Mcclure is a YouTuber and Instagram star. Ami is renowned as the mother of Instagram star twins kid, Ava and Alexis McClure.

She is famous for sharing her personal style, health, and beauty tips on her YouTube channel.

Ami Mcclure: Age, Parents, Ethnicity

Ami Mcclure was born on the 29th of January 1982 in Nigeria. She is of Scottish-Irish ancestry.

She attended Ewing High School in Ewing Township, New Jersey. Later, she joined Fairleigh Dickinson University in Teaneck, New Jersey to study accountancy.

Ami Mcclure- Professional life, Career

YouTube Career

Ami Mcclure first appeared on her family YouTube channel as the mother of the twins, Mcclure. Initially, the family channel was created on the 23rd of August 2006. However, the first video on the channel was uploaded after 5 years which earned 8.5k views on the channel. After that few animated videos were uploaded to the channel.

The channel went big hit when her twins’ daughters were featured in the channel. The adorable glimpse of their childhood won the hearts of many viewers. Those videos on the channel went viral in a very short time span and earned millions of subscribers.

In 2017, After the fame of her family channel, she started the self-titled YouTube channel. Through the channel, she shares the beauty tip and tricks, skincare, and fitness trick in the channel. Her first video on the channel, MY STYLE AFTER THREE KIDS earned 23k views. The number of viewers is increasing dynamics as the number of videos on the channel.

Apart from that, she is also featuring in the channel, the McClure ALGORITHM along with her husband and Playtime with Jersey along with her son, Jersey.

Instagram Career
