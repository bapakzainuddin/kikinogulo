Perfect Match Dom Gabriel age, Instagram, TikTok, height, and more

 Posted on September 4, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  363 words  |  Tandra Barner

If any Perfect Match contestant is going to steal your heart early into the show, it’s likely going to be Dom Gabriel. He’s not as well known as some of the other contestants on the show, but you might recall him competing on The Mole. Dom became the third contestant eliminated from the season.

In Perfect Match, Dom is one of many Netflix reality stars competing to find their “perfect match.” He’s been cast alongside stars from shows like Love is Blind, Too Hot to Handle, The Circle, and more. You’ll recognize many familiar faces like Shayne Jansen, Bartise Bowden, Georgia Hassarati, Francesca Farago, and more. Plus, Dom will also be joined by his co-star from The Mole, winner Will Richardson.

Watching The Mole, it quickly becomes evident that Dom is a genuine, passionate guy and a total sweetheart. A big part of the reason he wanted to win the game show is to earn money for his mom. We can’t imagine Dom will have a hard time finding someone special on Perfect Match. Learn more about Dom Gabriel below, including his age, height, where he’s from, and much more.

When Dom was filming The Mole, he was 29, so he’s probably at least 30 by now. Dom is from Toronto, Canada.

This Canadian babe is reportedly 6 feet 3 inches, which makes sense because he’s a tall guy.

Dom’s occupation on The Mole was listed as a heavy machine operator, it’s unclear if that’s still his main job. But he’s also the lead singer and songwriter of the band Don’t Call.

You can follow Dom @dontcalldom where he currently has over 42k followers. He’s clearly a very photogenic guy as the majority of his posts are from professional photography shoots and more.

Apart from Instagram, Dom is most active on his TikTok, with more than 600k followers. He’s posted tons of hilarious videos and content, including some “get ready with me” videos, a peek at his first red carpet for Netflix movie Your Place or Mine, and it looks like he’s Swiftie!

See Dom in action when Perfect Match premieres on Tuesday, February 14.
