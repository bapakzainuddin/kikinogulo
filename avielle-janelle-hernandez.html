Daughter of Aaron Hernandez: Early Life 

Avielle Janelle Hernandez was born on 6 November 2012 in Bristol, the United States of America. 

She was born to father Aaron Josef Hernandez and mother Shayanna Jenkins Hernandez. Her father Aaron Josef Hernandez was an American NFL star and a convicted murder. 

She grew up in North Attleborough, Massachusetts in a house worth $1.3 million. The 8,130 square foot house was bought back in 2012 after her parents' engagement. 

As of now, Avielle Janelle Hernandez lives in Rhode Island with her sibling: half-sister Giselle Guilmette born in 2018. 

Regarding her education, she went to Cheryl's School of Dance located in Rhode Island. 

Age, Nationality, And Height 

Avielle holds an American nationality and belongs to a mixed racial background. 

As of now, 8 years old stands at the height of 3 feet 2 inches (96 cm) and weighs around 25 kg (55 kg). 

How Did Their Parents (Aaron & Shayanna) Meet Each Other? 

Aaron Hernandez and Shayanna Jenkins were childhood sweethearts. They started dating in 2007 when they were still in high school. 

After dating for over five years, the couple engaged in 2012. A year after their engagement, Aaron was arrested on 26 June 2013 and charged with the murder of a linebacker for Boston Bandits, Odin Lloyd. He was released the same day he was arrested. Moreover, he literally came out within 90 minutes of his arrest. 

As the prime suspect of the murder Aaron, Shayanna, and Avielle were living a healthy life. In 2015, though only engaged Shayanna Jenkins took her husband's last name legally. 

Later, he was convicted of murder and was sentenced to life imprisonment. On 19 April 2017, Aaron Hernandez was found dead in his prison cell. It was revealed that the murder convict committed suicide by hanging himself with his bedsheet from the window. 

After Hernandez's death, his brain was analyzed. It showed that he suffered from severe chronic traumatic encephalopathy. 

A couple of years after the death of her ex-husband Aaron Hernandez, Shayanna Jenkins moved on. She started dating Dion Guilmette, a former boxer. The couple is engaged and blessed with an adorable daughter Giselle Guilmette.
