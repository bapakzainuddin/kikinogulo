Pregnant Ciara Flaunts Her Growing Baby Bump in New Adorable Photos

 Posted on August 24, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  67 words  |  Billy Koelling

New photos of her growing baby bump were shared by American singer Ciara on Instagram.

The star, expecting her fourth child, displayed her bare belly in images wearing a crop top with low-rise jeans.

This baby will be her third child with husband Russell Wilson, 34, who is a quarterback with the Denver Broncos. The couple already have son Win, three, and daughter Sienna Princess, six.
