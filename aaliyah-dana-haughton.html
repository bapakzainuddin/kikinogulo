How Aaliyah Dana Haughton Died?

On August 25, 2001, at 6:50 p.m., Aaliyah Dana Haughton died in an aeroplane crash while taking off. At the time of her death, the late Hollywood singer was 22-years-old.

Aaliyah had a private funeral Mass on August 31, 2001, at the Church of St. Ignatius Loyola in Manhattan with 800 mourners. Her body was placed in a silver-plated copper deposit casket and transported in a glass hearse pulled by a horse.

The late singer's family chose to release 22 white doves from the church's steps during her funeral. The 22 doves reflected her life span of 22 years.
