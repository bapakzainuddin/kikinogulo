Sienna Mae Death: The Truth Behind the Viral TikTok Rumor

 Posted on September 8, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1274 words  |  Kelle Repass

Introduction

Sienna Mae Gomez is a popular TikTok star who has over 16 million followers on the platform. She is known for her body positivity and dance videos, as well as her relationship with fellow TikToker Jack Wright. However, in January 2022, she became the subject of a shocking death rumor that spread like wildfire on social media

But is Sienna Mae dead? The answer is no. The rumor was based on a viral trend that involved users making videos with the hashtag #siennamaedead, pretending to mourn her death or react to the news. Some users even claimed that she had committed suicide or was killed in a car accident. However, the rumor is completely false and has no basis in reality.

In this article, we will explain the origin and purpose of this hoax, and how to spot and avoid such misinformation online.

The Origin of the Hoax

The hoax started on January 18, 2022, when a TikTok user named @joshuamartinez_ posted a video with the caption “RIP Sienna Mae”. In the video, he showed a screenshot of a fake news article that claimed that Sienna Mae had died of a drug overdose. The article was from a website called Channel 45 News, which is a prank generator that allows anyone to create and share fake news stories

The hoax was debunked by several fact-checking websites, such as Snopes and ThatsNonsense, which pointed out that the source of the news was unreliable and that there was no evidence of Sienna Mae’s death. They also noted that Sienna Mae herself had posted on Instagram and TikTok earlier that day, showing that she was alive and well.

However, the hoax did not stop there. On January 20, 2022, another TikTok user named @jamescharles posted a video with the caption “I can’t believe Sienna Mae is gone”. In the video, he pretended to cry and said that he had just found out that Sienna Mae had died in a car accident. He also showed a screenshot of a fake news article that claimed that Sienna Mae had been involved in a fatal crash with a drunk driver. The article was from a website called Breaking News 247, which is another prank generator that mimics the design and logo of CNN.

The video also went viral, with over 4 million views and 500,000 likes. Many users believed and shared the news, while others recognized it as a joke and called out James Charles for being insensitive and disrespectful. Some users also made their own videos with the hashtag #siennamaedead, either to mock James Charles or to pay tribute to Sienna Mae.

The hoax was again debunked by fact-checking websites, such as MediaMass and PolitiFact, which explained that the source of the news was fake and that there was no evidence of Sienna Mae’s death. They also noted that Sienna Mae had posted on Instagram and TikTok on the same day, showing that she was alive and well.

The Purpose of the Hoax

The hoax is an example of what is known as a “death hoax”, which is a deliberate spread of false information about the death of a living person, usually a celebrity or a public figure. Death hoaxes are not a new phenomenon, as they have been around since the advent of mass media and communication. However, they have become more prevalent and widespread in the age of social media, where anyone can create and share fake news with a large audience in a matter of seconds.

The motives behind death hoaxes vary, but they are usually done for entertainment, attention, or malicious purposes. Some people may create and share death hoaxes as a form of humor, satire, or parody, to poke fun at the celebrity or the public’s reaction to their death. Some people may do it for fame, fame, or money, to gain followers, likes, or clicks, or to promote their own websites or products. Some people may do it for spite, hatred, or revenge, to harm the reputation, image, or legacy of the celebrity or their fans.

Whatever the reason, death hoaxes are unethical and irresponsible, as they can cause emotional distress, confusion, and panic among the public, especially the fans and family of the targeted celebrity. They can also undermine the credibility and trustworthiness of the media and the information sources, and contribute to the problem of fake news and misinformation online.

How to Spot and Avoid Fake News

Fake news is any information that is false, misleading, or inaccurate, that is presented as factual and credible, and that is intended to deceive or manipulate the audience. Fake news can take many forms, such as articles, videos, images, memes, or tweets, and can cover various topics, such as politics, health, science, sports, or entertainment. Fake news can have serious consequences, such as influencing public opinion, swaying elections, spreading rumors, inciting violence, or endangering lives.

Therefore, it is important to be able to spot and avoid fake news, and to verify the information before believing or sharing it. Here are some tips and strategies to help you do that:

  • Check the source. Look at the website, the domain name, the author, the date, and the contact information of the news. Is it a reputable and reliable source, such as a well-known news outlet, a government agency, or an academic institution? Or is it a dubious or unknown source, such as a prank generator, a satire website, or a personal blog? If you are not sure, do some research on the source and its reputation.
  • Check the content. Look at the headline, the body, the language, and the images of the news. Is it clear, coherent, and consistent, or is it vague, contradictory, and sensationalized? Is it based on facts, evidence, and sources, or is it based on opinions, rumors, and speculation? Is it supported by relevant and credible images, or is it using misleading or manipulated images? If you are not sure, do some research on the content and its accuracy.
  • Check the context. Look at the background, the history, and the current situation of the news. Is it relevant, timely, and up-to-date, or is it outdated, recycled, or out of context? Is it consistent with other sources and reports, or is it conflicting or isolated? Is it related to a broader issue or trend, or is it a random or isolated event? If you are not sure, do some research on the context and its significance.
  • Check your bias. Look at your own beliefs, values, and emotions, and how they may affect your perception and judgment of the news. Are you open-minded, critical, and curious, or are you closed-minded, gullible, and complacent? Are you willing to question and challenge the news, or are you willing to accept and share the news? Are you influenced by your own preferences, prejudices, or expectations, or are you influenced by the facts, evidence, and sources? If you are not sure, do some self-reflection and self-correction.

Conclusion

Sienna Mae is not dead. She is alive and well, and continues to make videos and entertain her fans on TikTok. The rumor of her death is a hoax, a prank, a joke, or a lie, depending on the intention of the creator and the sharer. It is not funny, nor harmless, nor innocent. It is fake, and it is harmful, and it is wrong.

Therefore, do not believe or spread such rumors. Do not fall for or participate in such hoaxes. Do not be a victim or a perpetrator of such misinformation. Be smart, be vigilant, be responsible. Be a good citizen of the Internet and the world.
