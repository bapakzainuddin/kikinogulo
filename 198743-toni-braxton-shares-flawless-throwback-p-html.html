Toni Braxton Shares Throwback Pic from 'Secrets' Album Era, Proving Her Beauty Is Ageless

 Posted on August 16, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  448 words  |  Patria Henriques

Toni Braxton reminded fans of how much she hasn't aged as she shared a photo from over two decades ago from her most successful album to date.

If you’re a fan of Toni Braxton, then her recent post will bring you back to the 90s and her album, “Secrets." The singer shared a photo from that hit album which spans more than two decades.

Toni Braxton at the premiere of Saban Films' "The Forgiven" in March 2018 | Photo: Getty Images

The songs, “Unbreak My Heart,” “Let It Flow” and “You’re Makin’ Me High” were some of the hits that Toni dished as part of her album which she recently commemorated in a post.

The captionless photo she shared was a sultry image of her wearing a white top with one of its straps hanging off her shoulder. Toni sported the wet look, allowing her long curls to cascade down her back as she channeled a seductive expression. The photo proved she hasn't aged one bit.

Last year, Toni celebrated the album's 23rd anniversary by asking her fans to identify their favorite songs from it.

“Secrets” was released in 1996 and is the singer’s second album. According to Billboard, it peaked at number 2 and stayed on the charts for 92 weeks. Its other singles included I Don’t Want To,” “Love Me Some Him,” and “How Could an Angel Break My Heart.”

The singer earned four nominations for the album in the 1997 Grammy Awards. She won Best Female R&B Vocal Performance for “You’re Makin’ Me High” and Best Female Pop Vocal Performance for “Unbreak My Heart.”

Fans were undecided and couldn’t pick just one. One fan believed “You’re Makin’ Me High” was the sexiest R&B song ever, while another said it was a toss-up between “In The Late Of Night” and “How Could An Angel Break My Heart.”

“Secrets” is one of eight solo studio albums the "Braxton Family Values" star has recorded and remains to be her most successful. It even topped the charts in the Netherlands, Switzerland, and Norway. It peaked at number two in Sweden and the UK.

Toni's reign in music has allowed her to branch out in other fields. She has appeared in a number of television films including "Twist of Faith," "Toni Braxton: Unbreak My Heart," "Everyday is Christmas," and "Faith Under Fire: The Antoinette Tuff Story." She also part of her family's reality show, "Braxton Family Values."

There were recent rumors that the show will be signing off after six seasons due to a snag in contract negotiations. However, these rumors have not been confirmed or denied by the show's network, WE tv, or any of the members of Toni's family.
