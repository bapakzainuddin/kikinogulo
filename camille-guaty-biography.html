Who is Camille Guaty?

Camille Guaty is an American actress and singer. She is popular for appearing in Gotta Kick It Up! (2002), Prison Break (2005–2007), and Scorpion (2014–2015).

Her upcoming two projects, Casting Call as Sophia and My Best Friend Depression as Phoebe is in the post-production stage.

Camille Guaty- Age, Parents, Ethnicity, Education

Her birthdate is 28 June 1976 and she was born in Sunnyvale, California, U.S. as Camille Marie Guaty. The identity of her mother, father, and siblings is hidden from the media.

Camille’s ancestry is Cuban-Puerto Rican and as per her academics, she enrolled at Boston University.

Also, she went to a summer program at the Royal Academy of Dramatic Art in London.

Camille Guaty- Professional Life, Career

Camille Guaty is known for her acting in several movies and TV shows. At first, she was seen in the movie Chaos Theory (2000). She then appeared in Love Object (2003), Ghosts of Girlfriends Past (2009), Crush (2013), etc.

Her other big-screen projects are Cake (2014), A Futile and Stupid Gesture (2018), Lonely Hearts Club (2018), Nappily Ever After (2018), etc.

In addition, her fame increased when she was seen in TV shows Raising Dad as Olivia, The Help as Maria, and Prison Break as Maricruz Delgado.

Camille’s other TV acting credits are,

  • The Nine form 2006-2007 as Franny Rios.
  • Cupid in 2009 as Lita Arroyo.
  • Family Guy in 2008-2010 as various voices.
  • The Chicago Code in 2011 as Elena.
  • Scorpion in 2014-2015 as Megan O’Brien.
  • Daytime Divas in 2017 as Nina Sandoval.

Moreover, as a singer, she participated in a reality TV show named Popstars in 2000. Unfortunately, she did not win the competition but came as a top ten semi-finalist.
