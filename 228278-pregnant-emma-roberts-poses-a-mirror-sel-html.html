Pregnant Emma Roberts Poses for a Mirror Selfie in a Polka-Dot Baby Doll Dress

 Posted on September 1, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  451 words  |  Patria Henriques

Emma Roberts looked pretty in pink as she posed in front of a mirror while showcasing her pregnant tummy. The actress is on her way to motherhood, and she is having fun with it.

On Saturday, Emma Roberts took to Instagram to share a unique mirror selfie of her pregnant tummy. The 29-year-old mother-to-be is expecting her first child with fellow actor, Garrett Hedlund.

Emma, whose aunt is actress Julia Roberts, is still rocking a gorgeous blonde look. However, her dress and shoes were the highlights of the shot. The post is found below.

Emma Roberts attends the Vanity Fair Oscar Party in Beverly Hills, California on February 24, 2019 | Photo: Getty Images

EMMA SITS FOR SELFIE

Emma wore a pink-and-black polka dot, long-sleeve dress in the shot which saw her sitting on the floor with her legs outstretched. She rested one hand on the wall and used the other to hold her phone.

According to People, the "American Horror Story" actress was sporting a pair of Miu Miu chunky shoes. The black heels shone on top of the wooden floors.

"Baby doll dress has a whole new meaning," wrote Emma in the caption. The post received over 1.3 million likes. Her famous aunt, Julia, was among several celebrities to comment in the caption.

HAVING A BOY

The post follows Emma's announcement that she and Hedlund are having a boy August month-end. She did so by writing, "Me... and my two favorite guys," in a post cradling her baby bump next to Hedlund.

Emma included two blue hearts in the caption. Emma and Hedlund's relationship surprised many as Emma was previously dating and engaged to her former boyfriend, Evan Peters.

Emma Roberts attends the Vanity Fair Oscar Party in Beverly Hills, California on March 4, 2018 | Photo: Getty Images

BRIEF RELATIONSHIP HISTORY

She and Hedlund were first spotted together in March 2019 as they walked hand-in-hand in Silver Lake, Los Angeles. The two were reportedly friends before getting serious with each other.

When the media first learned of the alleged pregnancy, Emma's mother inadvertently confirmed it by thanking fans who congratulated her daughters.

Soon after, Emma put her baby bump on display in a gorgeous white off-shoulder dress. Hedlund, 36, also looked casual in a white shirt with a green overshirt and blue jeans.

MORE ABOUT HEDLUND

Hedlund is best known for his work in 2010's "Tron: Legacy" and "Country Strong," and 2019's "Triple Frontier." The actor was once a relationship with another actress, Kirsten Dunst.

The two were together for four years from 2011 to 2016 before calling it quits. Since then, Hedlund was not publicly linked to any other woman until he and Emma got together.
