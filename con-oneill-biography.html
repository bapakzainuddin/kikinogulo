Who is Con O’Neill?

Con O’Neill is an actor of British nationality. Con O’Neill is popular for featuring in Uncle, Our Flag Means Death, The Batman, etc.

His upcoming movie Rob The Last Planet where he will be seen as Enoch is in post-production and will hit the theatre soon. Similarly, his short films Pork Scratchings are in pre-production plus The Outing is completed.

Con for his role in musical Blood Brothers won Laurence Olivier Award plus was nominated by Tony Award for the same.

Con O’Neill- Age, Parents, Ethnicity, Education

His birth year is 1966 he celebrates his birthday on 15 August and he was born in Weston-super-Mare, the United Kingdom.

The name of his mother is Kay O’Neill and his father’s identity is Gerry O’Neill.

Further, Con O’Neill’s ancestry is English and as per his academics, he was enrolled at Elliott-Clarke College.

Con O’Neill- Profession, Wiki, Career

Con O’Neill since his debut in 1983, was featured in several movies as well as TV shows. His first project was titled One Summer (TV Mini-Series) where he played the role of Jackson.

But the role that gave him fame were Moving Story (TV Series) as Nick, Uncle (TV Series) Val / Gwen’s Dad, Ordinary Lies (TV Series) as Joe, etc.

Some of his big-screen movies are Dancin’ Thru the Dark in 1990, Bedrooms and Hallways in 1998, Persephone’s Playground in 2001, Telstar: The Joe Meek Story in 2008, and more.

Furthermore, Con O’Neill made an appearance in the HBO series Our Flag Means Death in 2022 as the pirate Israel Hands (Izzy).

Besides this, his brief roled TV shows are,

  • The Tunnel as Neil Grey
  • Happy Valley as Neil Ackroyd
  • Cucumber as Cliff Costello
  • Life of Crime as Ferguson / DCI Ferguson
  • Criminal Justice as Stone / Ralph Stone
  • Trial & Retribution as Alan Brickman
  • Real Women as Jo
  • Always and Everyone as Kenny Fletcher
