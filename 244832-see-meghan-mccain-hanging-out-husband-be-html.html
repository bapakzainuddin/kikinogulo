See Meghan McCain Hanging Out with Husband Ben Domenech in a Throwback Wedding Photo

 Posted on August 17, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  477 words  |  Kelle Repass

“The View’s” Meghan McCain recently took to social media to share a fun throwback photo featuring herself and her husband. The image was taken on their wedding day.

Meghan McCain, 36, married her husband Ben Domenech in November 2017 at a family lodge in Arizona. The couple exchanged vows in an intimate ceremony just before sunset at the lodge in Sedona.

The co-host of “The View” wore her gown with a white fur stole. Her parents Cindy McCain and Sen. John McCain, who has since passed on, both attended the wedding alongside around 100 guests.

Meghan McCain and Ben Domenech walk together as US Sen. John McCain leaves the Arizona State Capitol to go to a memorial service on August 30, 2018, in Phoenix | Photo: Justin Sullivan/Getty Images

Meghan’s representative revealed, at the time, that her father gave her away and she and Domenech were married by John Dickerson. After exchanging vows, the couple had dinner and danced happily together.

“The Federalist” publisher and Meghan’s wedding was planned by Jim Bullock from Events by Showstoppers. He revealed that the theme for the couple’s nuptials was a Western hunting lodge.

Their wedding included props like a gambling casino, poker tables, a cigar bar, roulette tables, and slot machines. The venue was decorated with unconventional décor that included buffalo and deer heads.

The bride’s bouquet also stood out as it included flowery and wild flora. The couple served their guests wild game and an arbor made of birch limbs and bleached elk horns sat at the end of the aisle.

The television personality actually chose to not have a traditional wedding. Going all the way with their chosen theme, Meghan walked down the aisle beautifully adorned in cowhides.

The couple was engaged earlier in the year in July 2017at the Mayo Clinic. The proposal came shortly after it was discovered that John was battling a glioblastoma, an aggressive stage 4 brain cancer, diagnosis.

This month, Meghan looked back at her wedding day by sharing a black-and-white photo on Instagram. In the image, she and her husband were seen smoking cigars while chatting to some people.
