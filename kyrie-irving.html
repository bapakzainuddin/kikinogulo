Kyrie Irving is an Australian-born American professional basketball player. He plays for the Brooklyn Nets of the NBA as a Point Guard. The 28-year-old has been serving as vice president of the National Basketball Players Association since February 2020. Previously, Irving played for Cleveland Cavaliers, Boston Celtics, and college basketball for the Duke Blue Devils.

Kyrie Andrew Irving was born on March 23, 1992, in Melbourne, Australia. He was born to Drederick Irving (father) and the late Elizabeth Irving (mother). Kyrie has an older sister Asia along with a younger sister, London. Irving relocated to the U.S when he was two years old. Besides, he holds dual citizenship of American and Australia.
