Noel Fisher Wiki Biography

Roeim Noel Fisher was born on 13th March 1984, in Vancouver, Canada and is an actor, probably best known for playing Mickey Milkovich in the series “Shameless”, as well as the role of Cael Malloy in the series “The Riches”. Among others, Noel has also played in the miniseries “Hatfields & McCoys” as well as in “The Twilight Saga: Breaking Dawn – Part 2”. Noel Fisher has been active in the entertainment industry since 1999.
