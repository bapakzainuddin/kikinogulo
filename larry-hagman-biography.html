Apart from working on ‘Broadway’ plays, he developed a keen interest in TV. Whereas, he also made his first TV appearance in the crime drama ‘Decoy’ in 1957. Additionally, he also continued making regular appearances in the TV shows ‘The Simpsons’ (1989), ‘Orleans’ (1997), ‘Nip/Tuck’ (2006), and ‘Desperate Housewives’ (2010). As a movie actor, Larry made appearances in films such as ‘The Group’ (1966), ‘Sidekicks’ (1974), ‘Mother, Jugs & Speed’ (1976), ‘Superman’ (1978), ‘S.O.B.’ (1981), ‘Nixon’ (1995), and ‘Primary Colors (1998). Therefore, his last appearance was in the 2013 film ‘I Get That a Lot,’ which released after his death. He earned a decent amount of salary. Whereas, his net worth was around $15 million dollars.

Larry Hagman: Lifetime Achievements and Awards

He was nominated in Golden Globe Awards, Primetime Emmy Awards, Daytime Emmy Awards, Golden Laurel Awards, and Soap Opera Digest Awards. Additionally, he won the TV Prize for Best Foreign TV Personality – Male (Bästa utländska man). Similarly, he won Bambi for TV Series International for Dallas (1978). Likewise, he won the Golden Camera for Millennium Award for Dallas (1978). In addition, he won Lone Star Film & Television Award for Best TV Actor for Dallas: J.R. Returns (1996). Moreover, he won the Soap Opera Digest Award for Outstanding Villain: Prime Time for Dallas (1978).
